# Employee Onboarding Process Designer

```
Design an effective onboarding process for new employees in my company. Here's my company information:

Company: [YOUR COMPANY NAME]
Industry: [YOUR INDUSTRY]
Company Size: [NUMBER OF EMPLOYEES]
Company Culture: [BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF CULTURE]

Create a 30-day onboarding plan that includes:
1. Pre-first day preparations
2. First-day activities
3. Week 1 schedule
4. Key milestones for weeks 2-4
5. Required training sessions
6. Introduction to company culture and values
7. Performance expectations and goals setting
8. Feedback and check-in points

Suggest ways to make the onboarding process engaging and memorable for new hires.

```
